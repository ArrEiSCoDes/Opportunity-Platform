# 🚀 Opportunity Platform

## Build Skills. Discover Careers. Create Opportunities.

![Opportunity Platform](screenshot.png)

Opportunity Platform is a resource hub designed to help learners, job seekers, and entrepreneurs discover opportunities for career growth, education, technology training, financial resources, and professional development.

## 🌟 About The Project

Many people struggle to find reliable resources because important information is spread across many different websites and organizations.

Opportunity Platform was created to organize helpful resources into one accessible location where users can explore opportunities and discover pathways toward their goals.

## ✨ Features

### 🌟 Opportunity Spotlight

A featured resource carousel highlighting:

- Career opportunities
- Training programs
- Technology resources
- Business tools
- Community support resources

### 🤖 Opportunity AI Assistant

A prototype assistant designed to help users find the right resources based on their needs.

Users can ask questions related to:

- Career opportunities
- Resume support
- Training programs
- Technology skills
- Business resources
- Financial growth

### 📚 Resource Categories

- 💼 Careers
- 📝 Resume Support
- 🎓 Training & Certifications
- 💻 Technology Skills
- 🤖 AI Skills
- 💰 Financial Resources
- 🚀 Small Business Resources
