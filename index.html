About Opportunity Platform

Opportunity Platform is a resource hub designed to connect learners, job seekers, entrepreneurs, and community members with education, career development, technology skills, financial resources, and professional growth opportunities.

This platform was created to help users discover pathways, build skills, access resources, and take the next step toward their personal and professional goals.

🏠 Connected Resource: Single Parent Career Hub

Single Parent Career Hub is a support platform created to help single parents navigate career growth, workforce development, training opportunities, and resources while balancing family responsibilities.

The hub provides access to career resources, resume support, training programs, financial stability tools, and opportunities designed with the unique challenges of single-parent households in mind.
